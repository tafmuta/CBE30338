Chemical Process Control

This git repository comprises a collection of Matlab scripts, Simulink models, and Jupyter/Python notebooks in support of a course entitled CBE 30338 Chemical Process Control. The materials are organized by Chapter following the textbook Process Dynamics and Control by Seborg, et al.
